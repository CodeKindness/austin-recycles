Austin Recycles!

"When do we put our bins out?" is one of the most common questions received by Austin Resource Recovery (ARR). Using device location or dropping a pin on the map, this app answers the question. Provided by Open Austin. Powered by open data from ARR.

Resources

Visit Austin Resource Recovery for more information about residential curbside collection of trash, recycling and yard trimmings. Additional services include sweeping streets, collecting dead animals, operating a drop-off center for household hazardous waste and collecting bulk items and large brush.

About Open Austin

Open Austin is a local, volunteer advocacy group. We advocate for open government, open data, and civic technology. This service was created by our volunteer hackers, and presented as a public service.

This project is open source, and you can participate by downloading at: https://github.com/open-austin/austin-recycles.git.

More information about the app development can be found here: http://atxhack.wikispaces.com/Austin+Recycles
